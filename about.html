About Me

This is what I do.

Welcome to my personal website! I'm Ishmeet, a curious and adventurous individual with a passion for technology and travel. I completed my Bachelor's degree in engineering from Manipal University, Manipal, where I developed a strong foundation in various engineering disciplines. Throughout my studies, I discovered a deep fascination for emerging technologies and their potential to shape the world we live in.

I am a casual gamer with a passion for PC and mobile games. With exceptional skills and a strategic mindset, I dominate the virtual world effortlessly. But gaming is not the only realm where I excel. I also have a keen interest in art, exploring various forms of creativity and self-expression. Whether it's sketching, designing aur editing, I find solace in the artistic process. When not immersed in the gaming or art world, I can be found indulging in my love for music. With an eclectic taste, I appreciate and enjoy different genres, allowing the melodies to transport them to different emotional landscapes. And let's not forget about their wanderlust! I have an insatiable desire to explore the world, seeking new adventures and immersing themselves in diverse cultures. From breathtaking landscapes to bustling cities, I like to embrace the beauty and diversity that travel offers.

Through this website, I aim to share my knowledge, experiences, and insights in the field of technology and travel. Here, you'll find a collection of articles, travel guides, and resources that reflect my enthusiasm for these two distinct yet intertwined passions. I hope that my content will not only inspire and entertain but also provide valuable information to fellow technology enthusiasts and fellow wanderers alike.

Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to connecting with you!
